# YouTube rubric — Bizzal Games (@Bizzal_Games)

Brand voice: "We love games... board games, TTRPG, campaign games." Daily D&D + Shadowdark.
Tone target: dry, mechanical, no fluff, wry veteran GM.

Score each criterion 1–5 (5 = excellent) and give a one-line justification plus a concrete fix.

1. **Title hook** — Does the title create curiosity/tension in the first 3 words? Is it specific to a rules/creature/spell payoff, not generic?
2. **Thumbnail clarity** — Readable at phone size, one clear focal idea, on-brand, not cluttered.
3. **Retention risk** — For Shorts: does it open with the payoff/hook (no slow intro)? For long-form: is there a reason to stay past 30s?
4. **Description & SEO** — First line front-loads the value; relevant tags/keywords; links present.
5. **On-brand voice** — Dry/mechanical/no-fluff. Flag anything that reads as generic AI filler or hype.
6. **CTA & series cohesion** — Clear next-step (subscribe/next video); consistent with the daily D&D/Shadowdark identity.

Also surface: the single highest-leverage improvement across recent uploads, and any content that is off-brand or repetitive.
