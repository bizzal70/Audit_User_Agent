# Instagram rubric — Bizzal Games (@bizzalgames70)

Bio: "Daily D&D + Shadowdark tips. Dry. Mechanical. No fluff."
Content is Reels repurposed from the YouTube Shorts pipeline.

Score each criterion 1–5 (5 = excellent) with a one-line justification and a concrete fix.

1. **Hook (first 1s)** — Reels must land the hook before the viewer swipes. Is the opening frame/line arresting?
2. **Caption quality** — First line works as a standalone hook; on-brand dry/mechanical voice; not a wall of text.
3. **Hashtag strategy** — Relevant, mix of broad + niche (#ttrpg #shorthand vs. spam); not identical every post.
4. **Cover/first frame** — Legible, on-brand, consistent grid aesthetic.
5. **Cross-platform fit** — Is the Short actually reformatted for IG (aspect, captions burned in) or a raw YouTube dump?
6. **Cadence & consistency** — Posting rhythm and visual identity coherent across the recent grid.

Also surface: the single highest-leverage improvement, and whether IG is just mirroring YouTube or adding platform-native value.
